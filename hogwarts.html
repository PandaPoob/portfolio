Hacked Hogwarts Student List

Mockup

Introduction

In this project I pretended to be a newly hired frontender for the wizard school Hogwarts. Therefore I had to build a student list from the given data and also add functions like filtering, sorting and searching so I could easily navigate the student list. I also needed to able to appoint different students to different squads or roles and even expel them. Furthermore I should also be able to click on each individual student card to see more details. If you are feeling rebellious you may want to consider clicking the button at the bottom of the page....

Process

Because this assignment had a lot of requirements, some even conflicting, I had to carefully plan my course of action. I did so by first making a list of all the functions I knew I needed and then breaking down the different functions into smaller activity diagrams that helped me maintain an overview.
Part of the assignment was that it was coded in vanilla JavaScript. I did so and separated functions as much as possible to make it more manageable.

See full activity diagram here!

UX

To make the student list more visually interesting and clear I created small icons using Adobe Illustrator. I made house crests for my filter, icons for active or expelled students and for the inquisitor squad and prefects. I also did this to make the whole student list more coherent. I also added a background color to the filter that is chosen to let the user know what filter is currently active. The colors showcased are also linked to the students' house. For example all the Gryffindor students have a red heading on their card and a red background in their details popup. I did this through JavaScript.
